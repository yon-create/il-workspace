# Getting Started — 3 steps

Welcome. Your AI team is already set up and waiting. You don't need to install
anything or know any technical commands. Just follow these three steps.

---

## Step 1 — Open the link we sent you

Click the link in your welcome email. It opens your workspace **right in your web browser**.

- **On a laptop or desktop:** if the page looks broken or won't load, install the
  latest **Google Chrome** (free, takes 2 minutes) and open the link again.
- **On a phone, or a very old computer:** open **claude.ai/code** instead — it works
  on almost anything and on small screens.

## Step 2 — Sign in once

The only sign-in you need is your **Claude account**. If a sign-in link pops up, open
it, click **Approve**, and come back to the page. That's it — no other accounts, no
passwords to remember, no forms to fill.

## Step 3 — Press Start and answer the questions

Click **▶ Start Infinite Leverage** (or it may start on its own). Your AI assistant
will greet you and ask a few simple questions about your business — in plain English,
one at a time. Answer them like you're talking to a person.

From your answers, it builds your website, sets up your AI team, and turns on your
weekly content and email schedule. You can stop and come back any time — your work is
saved.

---

**That's the whole thing.** Everything technical — the database, the hosting, the email
system — was set up for you in advance. If anything looks confusing, just type your
question to the assistant; it's there to help, and it won't break anything.

Need a hand? Reply to your welcome email and we'll jump in.
